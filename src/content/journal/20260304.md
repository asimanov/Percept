Toward the end of this particular session there's a decent amount of time for stillness or any other energy work that you might want to engage in. For the most part I took the time to rest while actively coaxing vibrations at the base of my spine. In the past this was rather difficult or impossible for me, especially in a state that is deep but not quite deep enough for further altered state work such as projection.
